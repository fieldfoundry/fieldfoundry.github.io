## Pricing

Your exact price depends on the complexity of the measurement and the batch size. We confirm the final price before work begins. All prices exclude VAT.

## Method Setup

New compound, new sample type, or no configured method yet? Start here.

| What you provide | Starting price |
|---|---:|
| A description of what you need to measure, plus representative samples | From £800 |

You describe what you need to know; we get the measurement working and make it available for repeat ordering. We measure a small number of your samples during Setup, confirm the method works, and report results. When you have more samples to run, you order Sample Analysis.

Our services are for research and development use. We do not currently offer accredited, regulatory, clinical diagnostic, forensic, or release testing. Complex measurements or difficult sample types may increase the cost. If we discover additional complexity during setup, we share our findings and discuss options before continuing.

Some samples need preparation before analysis. Routine preparation is built into the per-sample price. If your samples need a new extraction protocol developed from scratch (for example, solid phase extraction), that is quoted separately, typically starting from £1,200 for quantitative methods.

## Sample Analysis

Once a method is configured, each batch has a fixed batch fee and a per-sample price, both set during Method Setup. Here are two representative examples:

| Example | 8 samples | 48 samples | 96 samples |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Simple HPLC (£475 batch fee + £15/sample) | £595 | £1,195 | £1,915 |
| Multi-analyte with preparation (£950 batch fee + £50/sample) | £1,350 | £3,350 | £5,750 |

Your method may be priced lower or higher depending on run time, preparation, and number of analytes.

## What is included

Every order includes chromatograms and raw data files. Method Setup delivers a written description of your configured method. Sample Analysis delivers a results summary answering your question.

If you need a specific reporting format, let us know when you get in touch and we will include it in your quote. If your timeline is tight, ask about priority turnaround.

Mass spectrometry identity confirmation may be available by request for suitable projects and is quoted separately.
